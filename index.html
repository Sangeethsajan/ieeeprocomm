About PROCOMM

What is PROCOMM?

PROCOMM is an online technical project contest for the students to showcase their innovative, path-breaking ideas and take them to next level.

  • Who can participate?
    PG UG students of engineering stream can participate in PROCOMM.
  • Is it a team event?
    Yes. Every team should be of 4 members.
  • How many rounds are there?
    2 rounds; 1st round: Idea Pitching, 2nd round: Presentation.
  • Is IEEE membership mandatory to participate in PROCOMM?
    The team lead should be IEEE Member.

Institute of Electrical And Electronics Engineees(IEEE)

Institute of Electrical And Electronics Engineees(IEEE), association dedicated to advancing innovation and technological excellence for the benifit of humanity, is the world's largest technical professional society. It is designed to serve professionals involved in all aspects of the electrical, electronics and computing fields and related areas of science and technology.

IEEE Communication Society

The IEEE Communication Society (ComSoc) is a professional society of IEEE. ComSoc promotes the advancement of science, technology and application in communication and related disciplines. It fosters presentation and exchange of information among its members and the technical community throughout the world. To bring the world together in harmony through communications and networking technology research, application, education, and incubation of new ideas.
